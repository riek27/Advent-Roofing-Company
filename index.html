Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our products and services

Do you offer installation services for electronics?

+

Yes, we provide professional installation services for all electronics purchased from Albrens. Our certified technicians ensure proper setup, calibration, and integration with your existing home systems.

What is your delivery time for furniture items?

+

Standard delivery within Juba is 3-5 business days. For custom or imported items, delivery may take 2-4 weeks. We provide tracking information and will coordinate with you for a convenient delivery time.

Do you offer warranty on your products?

+

All our products come with manufacturer warranties. Electronics typically have 1-3 year warranties, while premium furniture carries a 5-year structural warranty. We also offer extended warranty options for most products.

Can you source custom furniture pieces?

+

Absolutely. We work with artisans and manufacturers worldwide to create custom furniture pieces tailored to your specifications. Our design team will collaborate with you throughout the process.

Our Story

Redefining luxury living in South Sudan since 2010

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in Juba in 2010, Albrens began with a simple vision: to bring world-class electronics and furniture to South Sudan's discerning customers. What started as a small showroom has grown into the premier destination for luxury home products in the region.

Our founder, Albren Malual, believed that every home deserves the perfect blend of technology and design. With this philosophy, we've curated collections from the world's most respected brands while also nurturing local craftsmanship.

Today, Albrens stands as a symbol of quality, innovation, and impeccable service. We continue to push boundaries, introducing cutting-edge smart home technology and sustainable luxury furniture to our valued clients.

Our Mission

To enrich lives by providing premium electronics and furniture that combine innovative technology with timeless design, creating spaces that inspire, comfort, and elevate everyday living.

Our Vision

To be the leading authority in luxury home solutions across East Africa, recognized for our exceptional products, unparalleled service, and commitment to advancing living standards through technology and design.
