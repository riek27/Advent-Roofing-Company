Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2003 by John Advent, Advent Roofing began with a simple mission: to provide homeowners with reliable, high-quality roofing solutions backed by exceptional customer service. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the region's most trusted roofing companies.

For over two decades, we've been protecting homes and businesses with premium roofing solutions that stand the test of time. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as industry leaders.

We believe that a roof is more than just a structure - it's the shield that protects everything you hold dear. That's why we approach every project with the care and precision it deserves.
